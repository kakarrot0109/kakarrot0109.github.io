MelvorIdle Mod管理

最开始接触发现玩不明白，当沉下心研究发现简直是个摸鱼神器，支持 PC\WEB\IOS 端多端同步，还处于新手阶段，记录一下该游戏的 Mod 插件。

Mod 列表

SEMI Core

Description

SEMI Core mod required to run all other SEMI mods. This one MUST be at the top of your mod dependencies above all other SEMI mods.

运行所有其他 SEMI 模组所必需的核心模组。必须置于模组依赖列表顶部，位于其他 SEMI 模组之上。

SEMI Auto Farming

This mod provides a fully automated farming experience, managing planting and harvesting while allowing you to prioritize crops, handle composting, gear, and potions, and optionally manage new abyssal farming mechanics. The core functionalities are tailored to support efficient and optimized farming, covering every aspect of crop production, from seed priority to harvest management.

本模组提供全自动耕种管理，自动处理播种与收割；支持作物优先级、堆肥/装备/药水配置，可选深渊耕种机制。核心功能优化耕种全流程，涵盖从种子优选到收割管理各环节。

SEMI Drop Chances

Displays drop chances on the combat and thieving modals

在战斗和偷窃状态中显示物品掉落几率。

SEMI Auto Master

AutoMaster will automatically spend down mastery pools when they are above 95% while training any skill. Uses tokens on skill usage, respecting SEMI All But One setting.

自动消耗 95%以上掌握池经验（全技能生效），技能训练时自动使用代币并遵循 SEMI All But One 设置。

SEMI Auto Loot

Loots gear on enemy death.

击败敌人后获得装备。

SEMI Auto Slayer

Automatically fight, skip, and extend Slayer tasks.

自动执行/跳过/延长屠魔任务。

SEMI Auto Sell

Automatically Sell items you no longer want from your bank or as you receive them.

自动出售你不再需要的银行或收到的物品。

SEMI Auto Smith Bars

Works its way down from the top of the recipe list to make all of the bars it can. Moves on if you run out of mats.

自动按配方列表顺序锻造全部可用金属条；材料耗尽时自动跳过。

SEMI Auto Open

Requires SEMI Automatically opens chests when they enter your bank.

自动打开当你获得的箱子。

SEMI Auto Collect Stockpiles

Collects your passive food from stockpiles as soon as it cooks.

烹饪完成时自动从仓库收取被动食物。

SEMI Save on Close

This script automatically cloud saves when leaving the page so you don’t forget

此脚本在离开页面时自动云保存，以免您忘记。

Show Item Sources And Uses

add loot numbers and chances.
add settings
mark done when shop or township
show numbers

显示物品来源和用途。

Multitasking

Allows you to train all active skills at once.

允许你进行多任务并行处理。
